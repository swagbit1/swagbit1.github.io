Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 19, 2026

ScaleTrack is built to be private by default. The short version: your data stays on your iPhone. We do not run servers, we do not have accounts, and we do not collect, transmit, sell, or share any personal information.

What ScaleTrack stores

The weigh-ins you record (a weight value, the date, and whether it came from a photo or was typed) are saved only on your device using Apple's on-device storage. You can delete all of this at any time from Settings → Delete all data.

Camera and photos

When you take a photo of your scale's display, the image is processed entirely on your device using Apple's built-in on-device text recognition to read the number. Photos are not uploaded anywhere, are not saved to your photo library by the app, and are discarded after the reading. The camera is used solely for this purpose.

Data we collect

None. ScaleTrack does not collect analytics, does not use tracking or advertising identifiers, and does not include third-party analytics or advertising SDKs. Nothing leaves your device.

Third parties

ScaleTrack does not send your information to any third party. Standard App Store transaction handling is performed by Apple under Apple's own privacy policy.

Children

ScaleTrack does not knowingly collect any information from anyone, including children.

Changes to this policy

If this policy changes, the updated version will be posted on this page with a new date.
